# Before / After

## Input

`sample_data.json`

Contains fictitious product catalog data:

- catalog metadata
- product categories
- featured products
- product table
- prices
- availability
- pricing notes
- order steps
- contact details
- appendix notes

## Output

`output/product_catalog_demo.pdf`

An eight-page professional product catalog PDF.

## Simple explanation

The automation takes structured product information and turns it into a clean PDF catalog.

Instead of manually formatting product categories, featured items, pricing tables, availability notes, and order instructions, the same structured input can generate a consistent buyer-ready catalog.

## Commercial angle

Useful for ecommerce teams, wholesalers, distributors, retailers, sales teams, and businesses that repeatedly prepare catalogs or price lists.
