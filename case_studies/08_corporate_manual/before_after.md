# Before / After

## Input

`sample_data.json`

Contains fictitious corporate manual data:

- organization metadata
- roles and responsibilities
- operating procedures
- policies
- onboarding checklist
- compliance notes
- appendix notes

## Output

`output/corporate_manual_demo.pdf`

An eight-page professional corporate operations manual PDF.

## Simple explanation

The automation takes structured corporate information and turns it into a clean PDF manual.

Instead of manually formatting roles, procedures, policies, onboarding steps, compliance notes, and appendix sections, the same structured input can generate a consistent internal document.

## Commercial angle

Useful for companies, consultancies, HR teams, operations teams, training providers, and organizations that repeatedly prepare manuals or internal procedure documents.
