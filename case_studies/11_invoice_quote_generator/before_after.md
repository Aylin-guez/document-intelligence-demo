# Before / After

## Input

`sample_data.json`

Contains fictitious quote and invoice data:

- company information
- client information
- quote metadata
- services
- line items
- pricing
- taxes
- payment terms
- approvals
- appendix notes

## Output

`output/invoice_quote_demo.pdf`

An eight-page professional quotation PDF.

## Simple explanation

The automation takes structured commercial information and turns it into a clean quote, invoice, or proforma-style PDF.

Instead of manually formatting client details, line items, pricing summaries, payment terms, and approvals, the same structured input can generate consistent business documents.

## Commercial angle

Useful for freelancers, consultants, agencies, small businesses, sales teams, finance teams, and companies that repeatedly prepare quotes, invoices, or proformas.
