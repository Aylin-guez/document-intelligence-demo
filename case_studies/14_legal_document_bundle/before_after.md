# Before / After

## Input

`sample_data.json`

Contains fictitious legal-style document data:

- matter metadata
- parties involved
- document index
- key clauses
- obligations
- review notes
- next steps
- appendix notes

## Output

`output/legal_document_bundle_demo.pdf`

An eight-page professional legal-style document bundle PDF.

## Simple explanation

The automation takes structured legal review information and turns it into a clean document bundle.

Instead of manually formatting parties, document indexes, clauses, obligations, review notes, and appendices, the same structured input can generate consistent legal-style documentation.

## Commercial angle

Useful for legal operations teams, compliance reviews, contract summaries, due diligence reviews, annex packages, and internal legal documentation.

Note: this is a document automation demo, not legal advice.
