# Before / After

## Input

`sample_data.json`

Contains fictitious HR employee data:

- employee profile
- onboarding status
- training history
- performance scores
- attendance metrics
- development plan
- appendix notes

## Output

`output/hr_employee_report_demo.pdf`

An eight-page professional HR employee report PDF.

## Simple explanation

The automation takes structured HR information and turns it into a clean employee report.

Instead of manually formatting employee profiles, onboarding summaries, training records, performance evaluations, attendance metrics, and development plans, the same structured input can generate consistent HR documentation.

## Commercial angle

Useful for HR teams, consultancies, training providers, OTEC, schools, foundations, and companies that repeatedly prepare employee reports.
